    The RTN Display scanner is an omnidirectional scanner that can read barcodes from any direction. Unlike traditional barcode scanners, which require the user to align the scanner with the barcode, omnidirectional scanners utilize multiple scanning lines and mirrors to capture the barcode data from various angles. The lottery tickets can be held in any direction (360 degrees) to get scanned.
